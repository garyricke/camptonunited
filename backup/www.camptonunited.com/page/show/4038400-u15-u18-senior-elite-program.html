U15-U18 SENIOR ELITE PROGRAM

High School Girls U15-U19

Campton United's senior team approach is focused on developing the player within the team.  While still focusing on individual player development there is much emphasis on a player's role within the team, the team attacking and defending concepts, and systems of play. 

Our High School level teams will participate in several College Showcase Tournament throughout the year as well as be participating in the top leagues at the local, regional and national level.  Our staff supports the development of every player in order to prepare them for the next stages of high school and college soccer.

LEAGUE PLATFORMS:

USYS National League (NL)
Midwest Regional League (MRL)
Illinois Women's League (IWSL)
Chicago National Premier League (CNPL)


EVENTS/SHOWCASES:

IYSA State Cup (IL)
IYSA Presidents Cup (IL)
Silverlake Showcase (CA)
Ohio Elite Showcase (OH)
Indianapolis College Showcase (IN)
Elite Girls Academy Showcase (MO)
Disney Showcase (FL)
PDT Showcase (AZ)
Eclipse MN Showcase (MN)
Puma Cup-Rockford (IL)
Inter Showcase (IL)
Elite Invitational (OH) 

CAMPTON HOSTED EVENTS:

Campton Indoor Winter Showcase (January)
Campton Girls College Showcase (July)

In addition, the club presents its College Night to aid our players in how to select a college, facts, and myths in the recruiting process, how to seek out financial aid, and how to market yourself to coaches.
